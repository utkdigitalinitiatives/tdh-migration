Wednesday 1st Nov. November 1826 The court met according to adjournment Present their honors, John Martin , Jas. James Daniel , Jas. Brown & Walter S. Adair Sarah West vs. Jno. John Walker Sn. Senior & Katy five Killer Try rights for a negro woman Quaity & her two children. The Court decide that Mrs. West is entitled to the negro woman Quaity only.
