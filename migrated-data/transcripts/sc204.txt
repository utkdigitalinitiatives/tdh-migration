Columbia 30th April 1844 Colo Colonel Robert Allen Dr Dear Sir by the advice of Govr Governor . J. K. Polk I inclose one promisary promissory note of Doct Doctor . S.B. Pillow for $20._ Due 4th Feby February 1839 which you will oblige me by collecting & when collected pay yourself out of it and send me the balance by mail Your compliance will oblige Yours Respectfully James Wilkins D Dear Sir Capt Captain James Wilkins is a neighboor of mine & is a worthy man & a good citizen. You will oblige me as well as him, by attending to the above request. Your friend James K. Polk Col Colonel . R. Allen Carthage Tn Tennessee
