Nashville Decr December 28th 1848 Sir Maj Major Reynolds Pay master U. S. A United States of America arrived here last night The Cholera excitement is not so great here as it was and I am happy to say to you that there has not been a case here yet nor has there been one in 200 miles of this place There was a man buried at the city grave yard yesterday, who died on a small Boat between here and smithland but he did not die with the Cholera, although it was so reported very Respectfully Jas H Kendrick
