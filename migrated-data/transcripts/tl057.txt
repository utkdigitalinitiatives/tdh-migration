Knoxville 4 April 1804 Sir Permit me to trouble you with An address which I beg you to forward directed to the inhabit people said to be settled and intruding upon the Indian lands. I am officially informed by the Agent of the War department that there is a considerable number of those persons settled on those lands, and I also understand that the regular troops are shortly is about to be employed for their removal I therefore deem it expedient to fore warn forewarn them give them information of the dangers they may be expose d themselves to, should they be found trespassing as has been to me represented I have the honor C et cetera J. John Sevier Colo. Colonel Hugh Montgomery Anderson County
