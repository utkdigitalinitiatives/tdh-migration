Nashville July 24 1849 Mr A Adam Ferguson Fergusson Dear Sir Excuse me for troubling you with this note but as I wish to start East about the first of August will want all the funds that I can possibly get you will therefore doe do us the favour favor to remit the amount of your account Times have been so hard here this summer that we find ourselves almost unable to make our arrangements for the fall trade we hope you will find it convenient to comply with our request and that we will receive a centimment of you favour Verry very respectfully &c and etcetera T L Legger Amount of owe $ 57 25
