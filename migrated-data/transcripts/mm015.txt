Detail of John Gerrard William De Brahm's map of an area of the Little Tennessee River Valley, showing the Overhill Cherokee Village of Tomotley. De Brahm stayed here as a guest of Chief Ostenaco in 1756. De Brahm was a British military engineer who contributed to the design of Ft. Loudoun, 1756-1759. This map is published in DeVorsey, L. Jr. 1971. DeBrahm's Report of the Great Survey in the General Survey in the Southern District of North America, University of South Carolina Press, Columbia, SC. This information was provided by the Frank H. McClung Museum. This document consists of one image only.
