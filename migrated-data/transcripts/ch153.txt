October & November Term 1828 Friday 24th The Court met and formed a quoram quorum Present Andrew Ross , John Huss , and Walter L. Adair Judges The Court have duly elected Elijah Hicks clerk of said Court. The Cherokee Nation vs Robert Saunders & Wayahuttah Wolf Prosecutor charged with stealing money The court acquits the defendants The Nation Chee, wo, Rul, ler pro. prosecutor vs Beaver Carrier Stealing- Court find the deft. defendant Beaver Carrier guilty and sentence him to twenty five lashes- and give him judgment in favour favor of the prosecutor ten dollars & Costs- Witness The Fencer 14 days $10.50 The Court adjourned till 10 Oclock tomorrow-
