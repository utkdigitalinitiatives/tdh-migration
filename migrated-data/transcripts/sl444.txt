Hickman Nov 23rd 1844 . Brother Daniel, If you have not yet forwarded the fifty dollars I wish you to send it to me by Mr Huddleston who informs me he is going up that way. We are all in high spirits as to the election believing that Polk is the President if he has not got Tennessee John Montgomery
