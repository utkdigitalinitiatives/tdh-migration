Knoxville 12 February 1799 Sir I have forwarded the accounts of King & Crozier for sundry articles of merchandise furnished for the Indian and Quarter masters Departments, and for the use of my office from april to December 1798, and amounting to four hundred fifty one dollars and ninety nine cents, which sum they request may be paid to their attorney Mr Mister . Samuel Meeker __ I am respectfully Your Humbl Humble . Servant David Henley agt agent war Richard Harrison esquire__
