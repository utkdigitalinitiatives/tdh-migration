Knoxville 16th Dec. December 1836 Mr. V A McBee Dear Sir I received your favor of the 8th Instant a few days Since. I accept with very sincere pleasure the appointment of "honorary member" of the Chi Delta Society and request you to tender to the Society my acknowledgments for the very flattering manner in which it has been conferred. You will please accept my kindest thanks for the manner in which you have made the communication- Most respectfully I am your Obt. Servt. Obedient Servant David Campbell
