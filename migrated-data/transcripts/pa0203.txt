March 5th 1806 Brother. as we have not employed Docter Doctor McNeil to innoculate inoculate our peoples, which is oweing owing to the empossibilily impossibility of the patients attending to the Docters Doctor's directions therefore you will please to pay the Docter Doctor McNeils Charges which is one Hundred & fifty Dollars & Charge it to the nation. we did not Expect to have seen the docter doctor here, as we never sent for one - we are your Brothers Chas Charles Hicks Interpreter The his mark x Glass Dick his mark x Justice John his mark x Bogs Col Colonel Return J Meigs Agent of War

Dr. McNeils order $150.00
