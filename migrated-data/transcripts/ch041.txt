F. Note from the Secretary of War to Rev. Samuel Worcester Cor. Secy. Corresponding Secretary covering a communication from the President of the United States on the subject of schools c. et cetera among the Indians. Department of War 3 Sept. September 1819 Sir, Believing that the society of which you are the corresponding Secretary, takes an interest in the subject to which the enclosed paper, See paper marked G. containing the views of the President, relates, I have taken the liberty to transmit it to you for the information of the Society. I am very respectfully Your Obedt. Servt. Obedient Servant (Signed) J. John C. Calhoun Rev. Samuel Worcester , Correspondg Secy Corresponding Secretary of the Amer. American Board of Commissioners for Forn. Foreign Missions.
