The State of Tennessee to the Sheriff of Rhea County ; GREETING. WE COMMAND YOU TO SUMMON instantly to appear before the JUSTICES of our Court of Pleas and Quarter Sessions now sitting in Rhea County at the Court-House in Washington to testify and the truth to say in a cause depending in said Court between The State Plaintiff and Laban Stuart Defendant on behalf of the State and this he shall not omit under the lawful penalty WITNESS, Daniel Rawlings CLERK of said court the 1st Monday of May 18 21 D Daniel Rawlings Clerk
