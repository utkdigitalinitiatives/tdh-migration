Creek Path July 23rd, 1813 Brother This is wrote from the request of Your Friend & Brother the Pathkiller (our chief) to acquaint you of with the rebellion in the Creek Nation , the principal Town Tooquachee (Toocabatchee) of the Creek Nation where four different tribes of Indians generally had their friendly conferences, is now destroyed & taken from the Big Warrior , the principle chief thereof, by the rebellious & hostile party of the that Nation. They have broke are endeavoring to brake break the chain of friendship between that & the U.S . & that Nation-The Big Warrior together with all the friendly Indians have dispersed from Too,ca,batch,ee to the Cowata & Caseta Towns, & it is said that the greatest body of the the Creek Nation have joined
