Knoxville 21 February 1797 Sir If conveinant convenient would be glad to receive the forty dollars I paid for building the home at S.W South West . Point . You are best acquainted with the form in which receipts ought to be drawn. Any one that you might think proper to send for the purpose will be agreable agreeable to me_ I have the honor to be With esteem & respect your obet. servt. obedient servant John Sevier Col Colonel . Henly Henley
