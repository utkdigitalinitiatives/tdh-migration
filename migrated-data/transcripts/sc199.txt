John Sevier Governor and Commander in chief and over the State of Tennessee . To all to whom these presents shall come. Here ye, That the name "Wm. Maclin" Subscribed to the annexed certificate is the proper hand writing of William Maclin Esquire, who is Secretary of the said State of Tennessee. Therefore all due faith, credit and authority is and ought to be had and given to his proceedings and certificates as such_. In Testimony whereof I have hereunto set my hand and caused the Great Seal of the State to be affixed at Knoxville the 11th day of July in the 29th year of our Independence, and in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and four. John Sevier
