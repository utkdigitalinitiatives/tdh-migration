26th January, 1798, Read the first and second time, and committed to a Committee of the whole House, immediately. A Bill Appropriating a certain sum of money to defray the expense of holding a treaty with the Indians claiming lands in the state of Tennessee . 1 Sec. Section 1. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 2 United States of America , in Congress assembled, That a sum not ex- 3 ceeding twenty-five thousand, eight hundred and eighty dollars, be appro- 4 priated to defray the expense of any treaty or treaties which the President 5 of the United States may deem expedient to hold with the Indians claim- 6 ing land in the state of Tennessee . 1 Sec. Section 2. And be it further enacted, That the sum aforesaid shall be paid 2 out of any monies in the Treasury of the United States not otherwise ap- 3 propriated.
