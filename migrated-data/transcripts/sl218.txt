Nashville March 13. 1843 My dear Wife I have just received yours of 8th inst instant which should have reached me on Saturday last The reasons you advance for not writing are not good, — as applying to your husband — they are perfectly inadmissible for if they hold good as to a definite period of time they are equaly equally so why you should not write at all — You also Say " That I should prize your letters very highly" Certainly I should, and do or else why should I press the request that you should write to me at least Weekly — An hour once in a Week, is Certainly a very small portion of time to devote to your husband, particularly when you have so much leisure — There is One Maxim time in life that which we esteem, love and prize the most, will Certainly share the greatest amount of attention — and on the other hand objects we Care Nothing ⁁ for are neglected — now if I admit my Mary's excuse for not Writing to me — I shall have to admit also (very much against my will, however) that she cares nothing for me — Do you see the dilema dilemma in which You place me — I cannot think the spell that bound me to thee; has lost its Witchery —

If so, Then words are not what they seem — nor professions of Woman tine — You are not very explicit in regard to the boarding business but have left it entirely to Me— then I shall do what I esteem (taking all things together) to be for our interest — I have no Idea of going to Alabama — and if I had not without you and James — you are too far from me now and to increase the distance over a hundred miles, is what never entered into my brain — I am about paying expences expenses here — and Cannot do much until I see Bank which I hope will not be long first — There is no one to whom I would be willing to leave James with more cheerfulness cheerfulness than your Mother — but I cannot reconcile it to my feelings to do so — and so soon as the weather and roads will permit — I shall send over or Come for You both — Have you seen the Comet in the West — it has not been mentioned by any astronomer, and Consequently its appearance is a matter of much speculation — We have an account of one which is ti make its appearance in the East on the 16th of this month — but the stranger in the West we Know nothing of — the Millerites will make Capitol, no doubt out of it —

Budd has been set at liberty and is now, somewhere a free man again — Maria Davis Was down One day last Week, to get Wedding apparel, for the Celebrating of hers and Packs Nuptials— and if she Can get ready will be married before her father leaves home for the south if not she will Wait for his return — The Weather has been so bad, that I have not been to Church for Three Weeks — Mr Cruarbins Workshop opposite Doct. Doctor Edgars Was burnt down last night between 8 and 9 oclock — all of his lumber together with a Considerable quantity of ready made Work was entirely Consumed — It is supposed to be the Work of an incendiary — all Well — when I see Em, the children &C and etcetera I will make Your love — are You Anxious to return or not — say in your next — If You are indiferent indifferent about it — as the river is in good order I may as well cut out at Once as to Wait longer —
