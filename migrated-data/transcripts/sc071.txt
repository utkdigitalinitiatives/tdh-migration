Clinton June 9th 1828 Dear Sir/ Having discovered that I can make use of more corn than I have ingaged engaged if you are willing I will take corn for the Whisky Whiskey you got of me and if you will take Whisky Whiskey for any more I will make you some as soon as I can please to write me upon this by the return of Major Mynatt yours &c et cetera William Stephen Major Phillips
