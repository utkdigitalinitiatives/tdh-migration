Friday 28th Court convened agreeable to adjournment Present their Honors John Martin &C et cetera Jurors G George Saunders It is the opinion of the Jury that the Ratting Gourd , shall hold the contended share that Archy Fields contends for, on the Nickajack road, George Saunders, Foreman We the Jury find that Jessee E Bean is fined by default to pay Susannna Otterlifter one Hundred & two dollars for the farm said Bean bought of Said Susanna, with costs &c Judgement against Jessee E Bean Dft by default, whereas Nancy Miller was plaintiff with costs of suit. Court is adjourned untill until tomorrow 10 Oclock
