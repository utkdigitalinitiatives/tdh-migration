1820 May 3 Know all men by these presents that I William Blythe of the Cherokee Nation doth Bargin bargain and sell to unto Henry Welker one negro man by the name of George Kemp aged about thirty years for the sum of five hundred dollars to me in hand paid and and I do agree to warrant and defend against all other lawfuls lawful claims for life in witness wherof whereof I have hereunto set my hand and seal this 3rd day of May in the year of our Lord 1820 Wm William Blythe seal Test testify R. B. Locke Roswell Hall

Bill of Sale William Blythe to Henry Welker for George Kemp
