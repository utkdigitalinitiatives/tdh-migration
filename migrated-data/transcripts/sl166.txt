State of Tennessee , TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING : KNOW YE, THAT reposing special trust and confidence in the patriotism, valor, conduct and fidelity of Joseph Brown of the county of Maury we do commission him Lieutenant Colonel Commandant of the 27th regiment of militia of this state; and do authorize and empower him to execute and fulfil the duties of Colonel of the said regiment, agreeable to law, a n d the rules and directions of military discipline To Have and to Hold the rank and command of Colonel of the said regiment, during good behavior with all the powers, privileges and emoluments there- appertaining. said Joseph Brown is hereby required superior officers' lawful commands; and all officers and pri- his command, are to be obedient to him as aforesaid. In testimony whereof , we have caused the Great Seal of the State to be hereunto affixed, Witness Willie Blount Esquire, our Governor and Commander in Chief, at Nashville the 10th day of September in the XXXVII year of our Independence , and in the year one thousand eight hundred and thirteen By the Governor , Willie Blount W. William Blount Secretary

I Joseph Brown Do Solemnly Swear that I will support the Constitution of this state and also the Constitution of the United States and that I will faithfully and Justly Execute the Office of Colonel in the twenty seventh Regiment of Militia of this state according to the best of my skill and Judgment So help me God Jos,, Joseph Brown Subscribed and sworn to before me this Saml. Craig No 14th. Sept. 1813
