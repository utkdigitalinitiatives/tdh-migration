Nashville Decr. December 19th 1812 Sir You will, without delay, make out to me a return of the number of companies in your Regiment, — how many men (including officers) are in each company; also, how many tents with, and without poles; — how many tin kettles, tin pans, and axes, and every other article of camp equipage recd. received for said regiment yours respectfully, W. B. Lewis A. D. Quarter master Richd. Richard Alexander Q. Quarter Master to 1st regiment Infantry } Post Script no more articles will be delivered to your regiment until the statement, as above required, be furnished. W. B. Lewis A. D. Q. M. Quarter Master
