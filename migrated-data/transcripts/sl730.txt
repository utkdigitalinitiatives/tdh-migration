Knox County personaly personally appeared John Johnston before me James White a Justice of the peace for Knox affxd affixed and made oath that on Wensday Wednesday the 28th of June as I was in the Woods tracking a dear deer I had shot, I Was fired at by Indians I think fore four in Number and Received a slight Wound in my side and was pursued some distance by them after the guns fired Sworn to and Inscribed this 30th of June 1797 John Johnson James White J. P. Justice of the Peace
