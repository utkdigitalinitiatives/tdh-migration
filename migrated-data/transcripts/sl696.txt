Campbell station 28 may 1829 Sir there is much disappointment caused, by being that governor Huston Houston has not as (Expected he would) anything, toward collecting information, from the different states, of their laws & regulations about their schools look at general Howards Howard's , Circular, printed in the Republican, a few week weeks ago, if you would take the trouble to collect this information, & lay it before the Legislature, you would do for the people, what no other governor of Tennessee has yet done what these will do, who is to be, I cannot say but were these whose situation admits of it, to them themselves more worthy I think the people them as their works deserved Samuel Martin
