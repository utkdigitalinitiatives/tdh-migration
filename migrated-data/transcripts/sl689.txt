Philadelphia Nov November 22d 1827 Dear Sir When you have examined the proposal, made by my friend Mr Mister Letrell, for establishing a Public library, in Tennessee & in every other state in the union, I can have no hesitation in believing that you will give it your most cordial support__ The liberal spirit manifested by your legislature in granting funds to the Nashville University , inspires confidence that, with your recommendation & that our your funds, this important national object will be accomplished & that lasting benefits will result. with great respect Yo mo obt Your most obedient Sam Brown Govr Governor . Houston Nashville
