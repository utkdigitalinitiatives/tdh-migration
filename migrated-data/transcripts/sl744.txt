Knoxville April 7th 1797 Sir I am Doubtfull Doubtful the prisoners is preparing to make their escape I am told they were heard at work last night appearingly appearing filing their Irons off I beg you to come over and examine them, and take the most affectual effectual means in your power to keep them secure in prison Your Hbl. Servt Honorable Servant . John Sevier Robert Housten Houston Esqr Esquire . Sheriff of Knox CO County
