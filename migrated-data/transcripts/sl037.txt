Maj Wm Major William B Lewis Head Quarters H Strother } 14th Dec. December 1807 Sir The Volunteers of Tennessee having been permitted to return to Nashville to await any order that may be given them by the Gov. Governor or the President; you will be good enough, if they should not be immediately ordered into service again to pay them for any complete stand of arms with its aventrements which they may deliver you, at the rate of ten dollars apiece. I am respectfully Andrew Jackson Major Genl General Maj Wm Major William B Lewis at: Dep. Q. Department Quarter Master }
