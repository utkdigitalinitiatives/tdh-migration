Knoxville 12 July 1800 Sir A Mr. Kilpatrick lately informed me, that he had applied to you as agent of for the department of war, respecting his son & Kyser, who it is supposed were murdered by the Indians in march last__ Will you be so good as to inform me what information you have recd. received from Mr. Lewis and the indians on the Subject, and whether or not, the perpetrators are likely to be discovered I have the honor &c and et cetera J. Sevier Colonel David Henly agent for the department of war__
