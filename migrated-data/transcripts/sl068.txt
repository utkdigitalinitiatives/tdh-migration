Woodbery Woodbury Ten Tennessee 9th of Febuary February 1844 Dear cousin I take the opportunity of writing to you a few lines that we are all well at this time hoping that thes these few lines will find you in the same state of health. I am going to school at this to Mr Ress I am studying Grammar Philosophy Astronomy & Arrithmatick Arithmetic I want to you to come and go to school if you cannot come to school I wat want you to come and see me very soon Elizabeth Sullivan sends ther her best love and respects to you I want you to write to me as soon as you can I want you to tell doct Doct Doctor Raggling that I send my best love and respects to him. Susan Burger
