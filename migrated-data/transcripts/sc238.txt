North Carolina Washington County } Ninion Hoskins complains To me on Oath that from Information from in a clendentant clandestined Manner that there Is a Certain Micager Crews in this Neighborhood with Two Negroes fellows & a wench that are Stolen From some Where the Owner yet__ unknown when & that the Alea Frasier Elisha Hoskins & William Kanady are concerned with him in sd matter These are therefore in the name of The state to commd command . you & every of you to__ Take the Bodies of the said Micager Crews Alea Frasier Elisha Hoskins & William Kanady if to be found in Yr Your . Bailiwick & in case they are escaping that you pursue from county to county from state to state as well by Footmen or Horse the also the sd said Negroes & them safely keep

so that you have them Before some justices or justices of our said county of Washington such other Equity of that they may be Delt Dealt with as the Law Directs & this shall be Your Wards for the same given under my Hand this Thirtieth Day of June 1788 To all Lawfull Lawful sheriffs & constables the & Capt.__ Rowland Jenkins Deputed officer__ Richard White

The State vs Crews Frasier Hoskins & Kanady Executed on Crews & two Negroes the fellow escaped Crews & wench bro Before Ricd Richard White first Day of July 1788 Rowland Jenkins Deputy Constable

The State of North Carolina Washington County Whereas Micager Crews Hath this day Having been Brought Before me on Suspition Suspicion of Stealing Two Negroes a fellow & wench they named Jack & Nanei and on examination He appears guilty of said charge These are therefore in the name of sd. day to comm commission . all Sheriffs &c Has this Mondy Monday of the said Micager Crew Together With the Wench Nanei Now in Custody Untill Until you shall deliver them into the Goal Jail of Washington District the Goaler Jailer of the said Goal are kurese commd. to Receive them into your said Goal & Safely keep as so to provide that you Have them Before the Judge or Judges of our District of Washington on the next court that shall be held for sd District & this shall be your Dist. for the same Given under my hand this first day of July one thousand seven hundred & eighty eight To all Sheriffs Constables &c & Captain Rowland Jenkins Deputed Officer Belonging to said District Richard White
