[Gift of Miss Emmaline Cockrill ] Copy of a Copy Natcheze Natchez Mississippi December the 1 1832 Know all men by these presents that I John D James of this city have this day sold unto Mrs. Sarah A H Allen of the city of Lafayette & State of Louisann Louisiana a negrow negro Boy named Feilding Blackwell aged about 16 years. for the some sum & price of Eight Hundred & fifty Dollars to me in hand paid by young D Allen for his wife person Sarah A.H. Allen & I for myself my Heirs & assigns will forever warant warrant the title to said Boy Fielding Blackwell against the claim of all persons whatsoever & free from the vices & malidies maladies prescribed by law Test Testimony Signd signed John D. James
