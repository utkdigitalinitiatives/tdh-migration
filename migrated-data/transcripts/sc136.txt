Alexand Alexandria . Nov November . 15th 1831 Dear Burford I this day make a shipt shipment . of 13 likely negrs negroes , 8 men & 5 girls no. 1. If they should reach the market before the passage of the interdictive, they will doubtless pay a handsome profit:__ Our subsequent course will depend upon the advise we receive from Mr Mister . Franklin__ Let me hear from you & give me the news at large Yr. friend JM saunders the whole number up to this time are 42
