Bank of the State Nashville 14 June 1826 Sir, You have been appointed agent of this Bank for the county of Smith to fill the vacancy occasioned by the resignation of Col Colonel William Martin Enclosed you have a Bond which you will execute (if you should think proper to accept the appointment,) take the oath prescribed by the charter and forward the Bond to Bank without delay, proper certificates are required with regard to the solvency of your security Respectfully C Cooper clk clerk
