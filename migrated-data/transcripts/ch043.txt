Letter from the Secretary of War to Mr. Hoyt , acknowledging the receipt of the Report of the Cherokee Mission, dated Dec. 12th. 1821. Sir, Your Report of the state of the school at Brainerd and of those which have grown out of it in the Cherokee Nation was duly received. The report is very satisfactory. The prosperity of the Establishment at Brainerd which has already Enabled it to Extend its advantages to other parts of the Cherokee Nation , affords the most cheering prospect of complete success, and should it continue will entitle it to additional aid from the government. I have the honor to be Your obedt. Servt. obedient Servant (Signed ) J. John C. Calhoun
