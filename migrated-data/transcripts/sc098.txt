Knoxville 12 august 1805 Dear Sir You will receive enclosed the copy of a letter from Colonel Scott of Blount County to the executive, and my letter addressed the warriors and chiefs of the cherokee nation on that subject You will perceive by Mr Mister Scotts letter that some of the Cherokees have committed a very daring insult, massacar massacre can not be passed over in silence- You will have the goodness to cause my letter to be laid before the chief so soon as may be conveinant convenient , in order that their answer may be obtained as speedily as possible. I have the honor to be Sir, with respect & great esteem your most, obet and hbl Servt obedient and humble servant . John Sevier Colo Colonel Meigs agent for the department of war
