Oct. 2, 1837 #33 Lead vale Leadvale , Tn Tennessee Oct October 2/37 Dear Sir Mr. Allen informed me that you wished to sell two negroes sandy & a girl. I suppose she is a likely girl. Sandy I know for $1500 It is more than I can give I will give you $1400 for them write me by Alford on the subject I would have been pleased to have seen you before I started but it is necessary for me to go by New Market tell Aunt Peggy good bye for me. Respectfully L. Lawson D. Franklin
