Rutherford County State of Tennessee December 2nd 1841 Mr A Furgusson Fergusson Dear Sir yours of the 23rd ult ultimo came to hand on yesterday wishing Some information respecting Jerry Smith s Revolutionary Services I was not born untill until after the revolution was over of course I Know nothing of his services I became acquainted with him after the war the latter named gentleman I am unaqcuainted with Mr Cobb I will refer you to John Patterson Esqr Esquire Snr of Summer County who was acquainted with both the gentleman if they did any Service its Known by him call on him and I think he can give you the information desired _ yours Respectfully Logan Anderson
