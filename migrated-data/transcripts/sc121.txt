Know all Men by these presents, that we John Shelby & John Scott are held and firmly bound unto John Gosset in the penal sum of One Hundred Pounds, to be void on condition that the said John Shelby doth with effect prosecute a suit, by him this day commenced against the said John Gosset otherwise to pay and satisfy all costs and damages that may be awarded for failure. Witness our hands and this 25th day of Aug. 1792 John Shelby John Scott W. Avery
