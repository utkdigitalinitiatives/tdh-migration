Nashville March 16th 1830 Mr A. Fergusson Sir yours of Febuary February 15 came duly to hand __ please send me the proceeds of Mr Green s note by the first safe hand and Oblige Yours Respectfully William Barker The Steam Boat Franklin will be in on at your City soon prehaps perhaps you will have a chance to send it by som some one of your a quantance O Green Dr $ 16,, & 8 amount to be sent to Will William H Barker $ 72
