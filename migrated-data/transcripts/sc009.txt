Territory of the United States of American south of the river Ohio , Washington County. Know all men by these presents, That we Isaac Denton George Nolen are held and firmly bound unto William Blount , governor, or his successors in office, in the penal sum of Six Hundred Pounds, void condition that there be no lawful objection why William Perogh Elizabeth Denton may not be joined together as man and wife, in the holy estate of matrimony. Witness our hands and seals this Second day of november 179 2 Test. Isaac Denton seal George Nolen seal John Sevier Genl General
