Chattanooga Dec December 24th 1839 To Mr. C. F. Charles Frederick Welcker Yours of the 10th Inst Instant has been received. in answer to which I have to say that I am hireing hiring the smith I have at $350, I consider him amongst the best of smiths. I cannot think that your woman would more than feed & cloth clothe her 3 children, of course consider it to my advantage to continue the smith I have, I hope this time by notice will enable you to hire your negros negroes to advantage Respectfully R. A. Ramsey
