Nashville Sept September 17 184 My dear wife picture of Henry Clay I received letter of 8th inst instant on last until your Mother with Susan Mary Jameson had left for a -land Campmeeting Near in Wilson Co County , — Mr Price down for her, and she took along for Company— Your Mother will be greatly supprized surprised to learn your robust health — as only a few days since, she was apprehending that your health was very delicate — I am becoming very anxious to look upon your sweet rosy cheeks, and I will here mention, that the swiming swimming in the head you Complain of, arises no doubt from your full habits — and perhaps in part for the want of proper attention to your bowels. — The Celebrated Boor have, said, "Keep the head Cool, the feet Warm, and the bowels open, and bid defiance to desease disease and the Physician You ask me to Come and see the rosy healthfulness of your blooming Cheeks for myself — This I shall do shortly, without accident— Mort was quite sick last week for three or four days — but my Pills brot brought him out and he is now attending to his business Louisa finds from the Square root I saw is in last and delivered your Message, she says, unless you return by the Middle of Novr November she will be off — but where too, she did not say — as she was interrupted — She also states, that you promised to board in the same house With her on your return, but as all things seem to Work well, I expect to remain where I am, and for reasons which I will Communicate in due Season— as I do not wish to be premature in such Conclusions —
