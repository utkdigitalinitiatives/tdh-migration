Thursday 20th Court convened agreeable to adjournment Present their Honors John Martin &C et cetera Jurors G. George Saunders Fore Foreman On motion, it is ordered that the proceedings against Richard & John Ratliff , wherein Lewis Ross is as plaintiff against Richard & John Ratliff Dfts Defendants be stopped untill until further directed. It is the opinion of the Court that Richard Rowe shall hold the negroes that Sarah Hughes and others contended for, Court is adjourned untill until tomorrow 10 Oclock
