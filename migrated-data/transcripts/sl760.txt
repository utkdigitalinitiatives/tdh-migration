Knoxville June 8th 1818 Sir I was appointed by the last legislature, one of the managers to superintend the removing the obstructions to the navigation of the rivers Holston & Tennessee (see page 161 late Octs) I am very desirous that some person be appointed that will attend to the duties as I find it out of my power__ you will therefore consider this as my resignation I am Sir Respectfully your Obt. St Obedient Servant . R. Houton His Excellency Jo Joseph . McMinn
