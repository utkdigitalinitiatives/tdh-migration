Washington County John Tadlock assine assign of David Taylor complains of Valentine Sevier in custody of the Sheriff overstrike for that wheras whereas the afsd. aforesaid Valentine Sevier on the eleventh day August one thousand seven hundred and eighty nine in the County aforsaid aforesaid did give an Order on James Stuart Esquire for the sum of twenty two pounds Virginia Currancy Currency equal to twenty nine pounds six shillings and Eight pence North Carolina Currancy with lawfull lawful Interest for the same from the year 1779 untill until paid, which said order the said David Taylor afterwards vz. on the day of in the county afsd. did assign over to John Tadlock all his right and interest therin therein , which order the said Valentine Sevier faithfully promised that he would well and truly pay and satisfy unto the said David Taylor if the same should be protested by the said James Stuart, afterwardsVz. on the fourth day of Sepr September . one thousand seven hundred Eight nine in the County afsd. he the said James Stuart the order afsd. did protest and altogether refuse to pay nevertheless the said Valentine Sevier not ignorant of his promise and afsd. hath neglected to pay any part or penny therof thereof although often therunto thereunto required. Wherfore Wherefore the sd. said John Tadlock saith he is injured to the value of one hundred pounds & therfore therefore sees &c et cetera Hamilton Atto. Attorney
