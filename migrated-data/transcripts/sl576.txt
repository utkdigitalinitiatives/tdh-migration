Nashville October 4th 1819 Dear sir I have got five plans of Memphis drawn in lepred stile style by Mr Lenori of Huntsville agreeably to our determination The price of the whole is $50__ of which Genl General Jackson pay $6.25_ you and your nephew $18.75__ and myself $25_ If convenient send your part, an I have
