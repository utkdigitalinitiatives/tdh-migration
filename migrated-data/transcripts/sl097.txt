Spring Hill Maury Co. Tenn. County Tennessee 3rd December 1847 Dear Sir: I have on hand some twenty pieces of rope & bagging each, a new & good articles from the factory of Mr Jas James Lugh of Williamson . As you are well acquainted with the quality, and learning that you were recently in want, I write to inform you that I will sell the lot at 5 per cent less than the current market price. You will please inform me, on the receipt of this, whether you will take it. Very Respectfully &c et cetera Gen. General W. White
