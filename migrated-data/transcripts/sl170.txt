Know all Men by these preasents presents that I James Hamilton of the State of Tennessee and County of Davidson have and by these preasents present quit Claim to Certain Negroes ( Viz Videlicit ) Jinney Suey Jainney Newchous Tannenole Senelaca Dorusendey & Omiah Certain Negroes Recovered by Joseph Brown from the Cherokee Indiens Indians a part of the property taken by said Cherokee Indiens from My father in law James Brown Deceased and as I Neaver Never have Recev.d Received aney any part of Said Estate since the Death of said Deceased and Will Not be accountable for aney Demand against said Estate I therefore quit all Claim to aney part of the said Negroes and Do Warrent Warrant and Defend the same from my Haire or assigne Heir or assignee to the other Ligation of Said Estate as to me or my Haire Heirs Witness my hand & seal 15th December 1814 Test George W Chartton James Hamilton seal Milberry Hamilton Samuel Tarner Acknowledged by James Hamilton July 9th 1815 Joseph B. Know all Men by these preasents that I William Anderson of the state of Kaintucky Kentucky & County Lexington Do as an Hair Heir of the Estate of James Brown Deceased quit all Claim to two Negro Wemen Women & their family (Viz Jinney & Suey & their offspring unto Joseph Brown and the other ligatures of said Estate as the said Joseph Brown has this Day payed me one Hundred & fifty Dollars it being my proportional part of said Estate the Receipt Whereof is hearby hereby acknowledged Witness My hand and seal 15th December 1814 James Hamilton Wm William Anderson seal Milberry Hamilton
