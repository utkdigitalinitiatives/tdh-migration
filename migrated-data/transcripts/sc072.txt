State of Tennessee Knoxville Sept. September 25th 1796 Honbl. Honorable Sir, By the admission of the State of Tennessee into the Federal Government, the appointment of a District Judge will be found necessary; I will gratefully accept of the appointment. Perhaps you recognize me. I have had the honour honor of a small acquaintance with you. I am with Sentiments of Esteem & Regard Your Obt Servt. Obedient Servant David Campbell
