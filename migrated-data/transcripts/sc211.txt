Mnt Con,s 10 miles west of Knoxville Wednesday evening Decr December 19th 1849 My Dear You will see by this that we are still making our 33 miles pr per day, should we continue through the remainder of the week to do so, we will be in the neighbourhood neighborhood of Sparta saturday evening___ Resting on Sunday, we would be in Nashville this day week, We have had less difficulty to encounter than I anticipated all well Sincerely yours Geo. George Stuart
