Knoxville June 30th 1812 Dear Sir: The United States on the 18th Instant passed a declaration of war against Britain to day recd. received a copy of it as passed transmitted by the Secy Secretary of State and expect to receive by next mail order from The Secy Secretary War respecting the detachment from the militia some time since orders to be held in readiness — I hope your Division on the detachment from it is ready — in haste your friend Willie Blount Major Genl general John Cocke
