The Quarter Master of the 1st Regiment Tennessee Volunteers is required to furnish as many waggons wagons as will transport the baggage of said Regt. Regiment from the present encampment to the place laid out this day for their next encampment. The waggons wagons to be in the present encampment at 9 OClock A. M. 31st Inst. Instant read to receive the tents and other camp equippage equipage at 10 OClock as the troops will March at 12 OClock Jas. James Henderson Brigade Quarter Master Camp Near Nashville the 30 December 1812
