Pension Office December 13th 1844 Sir I have the honor to inform you that the claim of Sarah Hawley deceased, has been examined. The certificate of the Clerk of the County from which the license issued should be obtained as evidence respecting the date of the marriage and the court should certify the day of claimants death and the names of the children who survived her I have the honor to be very respectfully Your Obt Obedient . Servant J. L. Edwards Hon Honorable . A. Johnson House of Representatives
