Hebron Church October 7th 1822 The Revd Reverend Andrew S. Morrison produced an extract from the minutes of the Presbytery of Union under the hand of the Clerk certifying his regular dismission from that Presbytery & directing Mr. Morrison to attach himself to the Presbytery of Abingdon where upon he was received as a member in good standing and invited to take his seat I hereby certify that the above is a true extract from the minutes of Abingdon Presbytery page 257. Saml Samuel W. Doak Stated Clk Clerk A. P. Abingdon Presbytery
