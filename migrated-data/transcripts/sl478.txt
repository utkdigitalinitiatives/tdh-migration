Tellico March 4th 1801 Sir_ I forgot to mention to you that great application is made for Cotton Cards for the use of the upper Town Indians - ploughs hoes &c etcetera __ The Glass has wrote me respecting- the Mill Stones being brought down to the look out Mountain where the Mill is errecting erecting the means of transporting them youll please to advise_ Taught I think had better come up and assist if you think it proper I will send him word for that purpose_ I am Sire respectfully your Mo Obt Most Obedient W William L Lovely asst assistant Agent to the Cherokees Colo Colonel David Henley }
