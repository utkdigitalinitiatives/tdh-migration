October Term 1827 Thursday 25 Court met according tomorrow to adjournment Present James P. Chisholm and others vs Alex. Alexander McCoy Damage to recover a ferry- As the Committee & Council have had the matter of controversy under their Consideration and never legally decided the rights of the landing contended for, we therefore are opinion that the rights of establishing, Roads, ferry's ferries & turnpikes is not visted vested in the Courts, but that the only authority must Come from the Committee & Council and therefore Cant establish the ferry to be rightfully possessed in either party_ and decide that each party pay their own Costs- Court is adjourned untill until tomorrow 10 Oclock
