As I have been ordered by the Secretary of war to retake the property of ambrose Harnage which has been Illegally taken from Sd Harnage by James Loreman at the Instance of Mr Joseph Lynch and as there are four of his negro yet missing ( viz videlicet ) Polly & Pheby & her two Boys Mr John miller Intr will proced proceed forthwith and take them wherever to be found and bring them to me at that place at Judge Brown s H Hugh Mongomery Montgomery 10th April 1838 I do certify that this is a true coppy copy of my orders John Miller U.
