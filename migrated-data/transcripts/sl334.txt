Nashvill Nashville tennessee mr A Adam ferguson Fergusson this is the 4 of June 1850 we ar are all well at this time my life is at stake and I wat want you to to send or fetch me two of the Best pistoles pistols that you can git get in nashvill Ross came in f the fall and thretend threatened to shut shoot be me twist twice or three times and drew a large hickry hickory stick on me and Swore that he would kill me we are dun planten done planting corn I believe that I shant shouldn't wright write any more at this time only that he drew his pistol or started to But he did not quite cumet do not for git forget to Bring them if you pleas please Sir yours and soforth Samuel D Hughes
