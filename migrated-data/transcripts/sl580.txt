Nashville October 1st 1825 Dear Sir Last night I returned to this place from the western Districts, to which place I went in company with Genl General Jackson & his lady. Mrs Jackson went to the neighbourhood neighborhood of Jackson in Madison County , to visit her only two surviving sisters The Genl was invited to partake of a public dinner at Paris in Henry County , which detained us a week longer than I expected, and kept me from leaving here on the 29th as I expected you will therefore have to give Mr Bledsoe another notice, so that I may attend which I will do on being informed of the time I could very much wish

that you could spend a day at my house__ or a week if convenient, in order that we may talk over your law business particularly the suit with Walton The Donelson suit I am master of__ I am not sure that I am of the other At all events it would be of great service to the cause to talk it over, examining each point critically__ This is the universal practise with Lawyers & their clients __ Writing is not sufficient At all times you Know me well enough to be convincd convinced that every exertion will be made on my part I transmitted you a deed for your interest in the Ramsey tract Have you received it if so please acknowledge the receipt

Page 3

of it by letter_ and as the deed discharges the obligation you hold of mine, it is nothing but right that you should deliver up that obligation to me, sending it by mail or safe private conveyance as soon as you can. Being in years less uncertain, it is my wish that no obligations should lie out, case of death__ I presume you omitted to send it by when you last wrote to me. I therefore expect to hear from you on this subject, being the only obligation I have out in the world Present me to Mr & Mrs Breedlove , of whose polite attention when at orleans new orleans I retain a dull sense As usual yos Resply yours respectfully J John W Overton Gen J General James Winchester }
