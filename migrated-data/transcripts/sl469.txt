Mr Jackons 24th Nov November 1795 Dear Sir Since I wrote you I hav have taken Bond of M- Searcy the Contractor- Andrew Jackson Anthony Foster and Andrew McMaury are his Securities It will give me pleasure to hear by mr Searcy who will hand this, how you are, and inform me inform me when you go to the northward I am with regard &c etcetera yrs yours JW Overton Col Colonel David Henley
