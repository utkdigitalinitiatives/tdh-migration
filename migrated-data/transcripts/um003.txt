Note from Uncle Jackson to get his watch. Mr. John Coffee Jr. will please to see Mr. E. Foster to whom I handed my watch to be placed in the hands of the best artist for repair, know in whose hand it was placed, apply for her, and if done receive her, with the bill for repairs, hand the bill to Mr. Josiah Nichol , who will pay it and send me the watch by the first safe opportunity and oblige yr your affectionate uncle Andrew Jackson August 25th 1832
