A MAP of The TENNASSEE GOVERNMENT formerly Part of NORTH CAROLINA taken Chiefly from Surveys by Gen'l General D. Smith. & others J. T. Scott . Folio 570. TENNESSEE. Map of the Tennassee Government, formerly Part of North Carolina , taken chiefly from Surveys by Gen'l D. Smith and others. Engraved by J.T. Scott. Folio. [Phila: M. Carey , 1795]. Very rare and early map of Tennessee, showing Public Roads, Indian Boundaries, Forts, Indian Towns, Width of Rivers in Yards, etc et cetera . This is an image of a map of the Tennassee Government, formerly part of North Carolina, ca. 1775
