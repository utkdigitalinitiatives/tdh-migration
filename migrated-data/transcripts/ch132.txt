Friday 21st. Court convened agreeable to adjournment, Present their Honors John Martin &C et cetera Jurors, G George Saunders , Fore Foreman Archy Foreman Ptf Plaintiff vs Michael Huffacre Dft Defendant for breaking the Cherokee law Judgement confessed $10_00 with the costs of suit $4_50 Cherokee Nation Ptf vs Gahcowe Dft Indictment for Murder of Ezekiel Walkingstick s brother, We the Judges of the Supreme Court, find no bill against the Dft Gahcowe, Given from, under my hand at New Town this 21st of October 1825 JMartin, C,S,C unknown Jessee Bushy Head Ptf vs Samuel Henry Dft For breaking the Cherokee by selling spiritous liquors It is the opinion of the jury that Samuel Henry has violated the Cherokee law & be fined $100 George Saunders, Foreman. Court is adjourned untill until tomorrow 10 Oclock.
