Ho House of Repr Representatives Feb February . 4. 1833. My Dear little Daughter Yours of the 6th ult ultimo . I have recd received . I am glad to hear my child that you are learning so fast and I see you are improving much in Writing you must persevere and try to excell every girl at school in learning & goodness you must be careful and neat in your attire affectionate to your sister & obedient to your Mother. & preceptor I shall leave here this day four weeks. for Home. I have not had very good health this Winter but am now nearly well. Yr Your . Father W Fitzgerald Miss Adeline Fitzgerald Dresden Tennessee
