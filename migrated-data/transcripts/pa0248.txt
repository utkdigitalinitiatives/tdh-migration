State of Tennessee McMinn County Be it known that I John Camp a justice of the Peace for the County aforesaid have called before me at my house in said County on this the 16th day of December 1836 and proceed to take the Deposition of John Austin a citizen of Calhoun in P county who after being duly sworn according to Law departh and says__ that Question 1st Mr Mister Austin are you acquainted with James Foreman & Anderson Springstin native Cherokees of the Cherokee nation who were charged with the murder of Jno John Walker Jr Junior & held at Athens Court Tennessee , in 1834__ If so state if the notes here presented in the note given by them to G.W. Churchill & Spencer Jasuagin __ and what was it given for__ & state all about it__ I am acquainted with the Foreman , and was present when the note here me, & answered to this paper, for twelve hundred Dollars dated 31st October 1834 __ & signed by James C. Foreman & Anderson springstine payable to GW Churchill & spencer Jarnagin , was executed and I signed my name as a witness to said note__ after they signed & acknowledged it in my presence__ They employed Churchill & Jarnagin as Lawyers to defend them against a charge of the state as them for the murder of Jus Tralker as a native, & executed this note in my presence to Lawyers for their fees incase in case and I was present in the Court house and heard Lawyers defend their case. upon a plea to the jurisdiction of

Court, and they labored hard in Cause for the Indian question, & argued the cause for several days and succeeded, in sustaining thier plea__and the judge gave his opinion in faver of the pleas that the Courts of Tennessee had no jurisdition of the case said note is just, & the Indians aforesaid value for __ note as aforeaid__ The attorney General appealed ffrom the Judges opinion, to the Supreme Court of Tennessee where the cause was again argued as I have understood__ but the Supreme Court decided against the Indians pleas & the cause is now gone to the Supreme Court of the U I have no knowledge of the fact nor do I believe the U states United States __. further this Defenent saith not note was ever paid.__ John Austin State of Tennessee McMinn County I John Camp a justice of the Peace for County, do hereby certify that John Austin signed & swore to this Deposition & note described & enclosed as stated above before me this 16th day of December 1836 __ and I certify that I am acquainted with the Character of john Austin the Defenant defendant , and state that he is a respectable man & entitled to full credit__ Jno Camp justice of the peace for the County aforesaid
