Friday Octo. October 31st Court met according to adjournment present the honorable Judges- Walter Adair vs George Hicks an action for Collecting unlawfull unlawful fees the Court finds a verdict for the defendant Archillah Smith vs Andrew Ross an action for a horse the Court finds for the defendant and Costs for the plaintiffs Witnesses Goose $9.75 Peter 3.00 Pigeon 3.00 Young Hogg Ned Court is adjourned to 10 Oclock tomorrow morning
