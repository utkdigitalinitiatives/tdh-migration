Executive Department Tahlequah Nov. 18th. 1857 Colo. Colonel George Butler U.S. Agent Sir,, In compliance with the requirements of the enclosed Copy of an Act of the National Council; It has you will perceive that it has become my duty again, "to call upon you, as, the Agent of the United States , for the immediate removal of the intruders upon the Neutral Lands" I am Sir, Very respectfully Your Obt. Servt. Obedient Servant John Ross Prinl. Principal Chief Cherokee Nation
