Winchester Tenn. Tennessee August 19th 1836 Gentlemen The present is to say that we wish to know what disposition you made with the four cotton gins we sent you last December. your Recpt. Receipt calls for 3 @ 200$ and one at 125$ We should be obliged if you write us on Recpt. Receipt of this whether you have sold any of the above gins as yet and whether we can receive any of the money for them this ensuing fall Your Friends Respectfully James R. Hobbs & Co. Company Mess.
