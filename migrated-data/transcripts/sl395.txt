Dover Nov November 12 1832 My Dear All here are well__ I wish you would move to our farm as quick as possible the Cholera is spreading in all the little towns carried there by persons from the river__ look at the Republican of the 8th Inst. Instant you will see Dr. Drakes directions & attend to them so soon as the Chimny is finished move all out there & have no communication with Town go nor send to any place where persons are suddenly taken sick
