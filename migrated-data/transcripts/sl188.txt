Columbia Tenn Tennessee Nov November 15th 1849 Dear Grand father I reached here to day to dinner— design going out to Grand mother Mills anxious in the afternoon — will continue my journey to M' boro Murfreesboro tomorrow, I should be pleased indeed to visit you, but cannot do so, as my time is limited — possibly I may do so on my return home, if I come through here — but I shall most likely return by Nashville — All were very well when I left, to day week — Mr Brown was reelected by a large majority — the Democratic Ticket prevailed throughout — Present me kindly to Mr Lane & family & for yourself accept the highest regard of Yours Truly WHD Wendel
