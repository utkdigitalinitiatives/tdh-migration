Carthage July 21s 1848 I J James C Jones Govenor Governor of Tenn Tennessee I hereby certify that the company of Volunteers called the Mountain Boys attached to the 52d regiment of the Tennessee militia is in complete uniform concisting consisting of forty five rank & file given under my Kand in Carthage on the 21s July 1843 Albert H Ross Colo Colonel commdt commandant of 52nd Regiment of T.M Tennessee Militia
