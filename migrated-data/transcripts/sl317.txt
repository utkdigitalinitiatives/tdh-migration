N ashville 19th Feby February 1846 Sir & Brother At the next regular Meeting of your Lodge you Will please cause an action to be had on the New Constitution submitted by the Grand Lodge at its last Annual Meeting to the Subordinate Lodges for their approval or rejection it is all important that this should be attended to at as early a day as possible, if a sufficient number should adopt it the Grand Master Will issue his Circular to that Effect that the same may be for the government of the Lodges. you will please address your Letter to me, as soon as you have taken action as aforesaid Very Respectfully John S Dashiell Grand Secty Secretary Grand Lodge of Tenn Tennessee
