Mr Speaker and Gentlemen of the House of Representatives The Senate do not concur with you in referring the petition from Sundry inhabitants of Mero District , praying that the Road from South West Point to Sumner County be repaired, & propose that it be refered referred to a joint committee of the two houses & name on their part Mr Douglass & Mr. Rutledge To which they request your concurrence Wm William Blount S S By order, G George Roulstone C. S. Clerk of the Senate
