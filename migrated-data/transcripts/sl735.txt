Knoxville 6 May 1796 Dr Dear Sir I am under expectation that I shall be ready to sit out for washington , some time to day or early tomorrow morning shall I suppose be absent from this place, three or four weeks; In the mean time I beg you to have a watchfull watchful eye towards the frontiers, and their your neighbours neighbors the Cherokee s. If any thing of moment should occur, let me know by express as soon as possible I am respectfully sir your mo obedt sert most obedient servant John Sevier Circular Colonels White & Kelly and Weir
