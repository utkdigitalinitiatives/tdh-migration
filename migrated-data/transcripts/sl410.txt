Marshall County Miss Mississippi March 5th 1838 Dear Son We are all in Common health at presant present and hope this will find you well also times are flourishing in this Country the land office is Closed at presant but will be oppend opened again shortly, there is a good ade of vacant land yet, and Mr Greer says if you will send him the Silver he will enter one or more quarter Sections of Land at 50 cents per acre that be sold for $2.50 or $3.00 which would be a large profitable investment these hard times your relations are all in Common health you must Come down this Spring and us. nothing more at presant but remain your affectionate Mother untill until death Rachael Greer
