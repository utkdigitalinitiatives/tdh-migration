No Number 278 Dollars 137.8 William Blount Governor in and over the Territory of the United States south of the river Ohio To David Henley Esquire Agent for the Department of war Pay to William Rickard Pay. Master pro tem to the Troops in the Territory aforesaid one hundred and thirty seven dollars eight cents out of the money on your hands appropriated for the defensive protection of the frontiers for the pay of a Department of Mounted Infantry of Mero District Colonel James Ford s Regiment under the command of Captain George Briscoe from the fourteenth February , to the twentieth February 1794 agreeably to pay roll and your report thereon of this date, for which this shall be your warrant. Given at Knoxville under my hand and seal this 2d July 1795 Wm William Blount By the Governor Chas Charles McClung Pro Secretary Recorded in the Secretary's Office July 2d 1795 Chas MClung Pro. Secretary
