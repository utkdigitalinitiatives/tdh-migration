Mount Pleasant Oct October 7th 34 Dear Mother We reached this in safety on Thursday Morning last but did not find all well — Brothers two sons Ge orge were both attacked the morning of our arrival and have been quite sick until Yesterday, when they seemed much better — the others of the family are well with the exception old Aunt Edy — Penny's Mother — who has something like acute Rheumatism in her left wrist and arm I have undertaken to cure it with my new mixture, of which You recollect I dreamed while at Valmont — before I left Nashville Mortimer prepared for me a half gallon which I have with me — have found great relief myself from the use of it only three or four times. and am confident I shall make a cure in both cases — There has been much sickness Throughout the whole country and many of my acquaintances and very worthy citizens have give long home to try the realities of another what is all the wealth and honour honor of ld compared to the immortality of the soul true religion alone that refines & elevates the religion that cherishes virtue and checks tis religion that raises man above
